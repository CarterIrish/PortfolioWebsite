Spring Fling: To Japan

About The Trip

Osaka is an beautiful city that combines the traditions of japan with the evolving world. Osaka makes use of its wonderful nature and commercialism to attract millions of people each year and further display the combonation of hyper modern and tradition. The city still retains many shrines from its past and blends them together with the many skyscrapers and modern buildings of the 21st century. This trip to Osaka will guide you through the many destinations and sights of Osaka, allowing all attendees to enjoy the beautiful history and tasty food.
